DAMAGED PRODUCT POLICY — 2025 Revision

1. Definition of Damage:
   • Minor Damage – cosmetic damage that does not affect item usability
     (examples: scratches, dents smaller than 1 cm, faded print)
   • Major Damage – functional or structural damage that prevents usage
     (examples: broken screen, cracked component, water damage)

2. Resolutions:
   • Minor Damage
       - Customer eligible for replacement only if under warranty
       - Refund applicable if issue reported within 7 days of purchase.
   • Major Damage
       - Customer eligible for immediate replacement or refund
       - Item return is mandatory before refund is processed

3. Evidence Requirements:
   • Photos are required in all cases

4. Shipping:
   • If the product is damaged upon arrival, return shipping is free
   • Self-damage voids warranty (cracks due to drops, misuse, or tampering)
