RETURN POLICY — 2025

1. Standard Return Window:
   • Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
   • Return window for electronics is 15 days.
   • Apparel and accessories must have tags intact and be unused.

2. Refund & Replacement Rules:
   • Refunds are issued only after the returned item is inspected and approved.
   • Replacements can be issued before inspection only for premium customers.
   • Return shipping is free when the issue is reported within 48 hours of delivery.

3. Special Event Orders:
   • Products purchased during flash sales are eligible only for replacement, not refund.
   • Seasonal clearance sales permit refund or replacement only if the item arrived defective.

4. Exclusions:
   • Digital goods are non-returnable.
   • Perishable groceries cannot be returned; refunds may apply based on eligibility.
   • Personalized and monogrammed items are non-returnable unless defective.

5. Evidence Requirements:
   • Customers must provide photos/videos if the reason for return is damage or defective.

6. Processing Timelines:
   • Once the warehouse receives the item, inspection is completed within 5 business days.
   • Refunds are initiated within 48 hours after inspection approval.
   • Replacements ship within 72 hours after approval.
